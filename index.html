About Me
My professional career began in medical graduate education. During that time, I gained invaluable experience in healthcare administration with exposure to various medical programs. In the years to follow, I explored careers geared towards gaining knowledge and experience in research, healthcare admin, and project coordination. In 2014, I relocated to San Diego, CA where I continued working in research. Though I had enjoyed many years of working in research, I found that my interest in clinical research had become less engaging or fullfilling. That's when my search for an new goal began and after much consideration of various new career options, I found that computer coding was of the greatest appeal. The most attractive aspect of programming for me is the logic, reasoning, and challenge of problem solving. This refreshing journey holds promise of a fullfilling and challenging career with endless opportunity to grow and learn.
